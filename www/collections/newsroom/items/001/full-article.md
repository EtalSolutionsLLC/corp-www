# ReviewNudge to Launch Internationally July 20, Beginning in the Netherlands

Customer-review workspace introduces an 11-language launch experience and opens region by region through 8:00 a.m. Pacific.

LONG BEACH, Calif. - July 17, 2026 - PRLog -- ReviewNudge, a workspace for organizing customer-review outreach, launches internationally Monday, July 20. It begins in the Netherlands at 8:00 a.m. CEST and moves westward. The final wave opens at 8:00 a.m. PDT.

ReviewNudge originally targeted July 15, but founder David Omer postponed the launch after finding it lacked meaningful global language support. He added several days to make the launch more than a symbolic acknowledgment of those it hopes to serve.

"On our original launch date, I realized that acknowledging a region while disregarding the language spoken by the people who live there sends the wrong message," said Omer.

"Language is one of the most powerful tools humans have to connect, recognize one another and show respect. I could not put my name on an international launch that treated it as an afterthought."

ReviewNudge gives customer-facing teams one place for review links, outreach, follow-ups, status and team visibility. It shows what happened, what hasn't yet, and who is handling it.

ReviewNudge grew from Omer's micro-SaaS framework and his conclusion that many sites have become difficult to use. Customer reviews can make usability a public conversation, but useful feedback requires participation. ReviewNudge makes the first step - asking - easier.

The launch supports Dutch, English, Simplified Chinese, Hindi, Spanish, Arabic, French, Bengali, Brazilian Portuguese, Indonesian and Urdu.

Five minutes before the Netherlands launch, the Coming Soon page will give way to ReviewNudge in dark launch mode. Visitors will see the rollout and countdown while other product functions remain gated. At 8:00 a.m. CEST, access opens in the Netherlands and regions to its east past their Monday launch window.

The rollout moves westward through the United Kingdom, South America and the United States, then Eastern, Central, Mountain and Pacific time. As each region opens, the display shrinks to a compact status indicator. Hawaii joins the final Pacific wave, giving the rollout one clear finish.

*"People using ReviewNudge should be able to focus on the business they know, rather than becoming technical support for another piece of software," Omer said. "It should simply work."*

ReviewNudge will be available beginning July 20 at [reviewnudge.etal.solutions](https://reviewnudge.etal.solutions/).

## About ReviewNudge

ReviewNudge is a customer-review workspace from Et al Solutions LLC. It helps businesses organize review links, outreach, follow-up activity, customer status and team visibility in one straightforward workspace.
